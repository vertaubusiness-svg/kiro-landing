Why we built Kiro

Small shops lose the most to a ringing phone — and can afford it the least.

I used to work the counter at a busy bakery café. The phone rang more than you'd think, and every ring was the same decision: stop what I'm doing and pick up, or keep serving the person in front of me? You never know which call is the one that turns into a real order — so you can't just ignore it.

A big shop has enough hands to do both. A small one doesn't.

That's the whole reason Kiro exists. When you don't have to choose between the phone and the work in front of you, you get your focus back — and no call goes unanswered. The enterprise systems that solve this cost thousands and are built for chains. I wanted the one-person shop to have that same "never miss a call" power, at a price a small business can actually carry.

— The founder of Kiro

Straight answers

The questions owners actually ask.

Will Kiro replace my staff?
No. Kiro catches the calls you'd otherwise miss while your hands are full, and hands off to your team the moment a caller wants a person. Your staff keep doing what they do best — Kiro just makes sure the phone always gets answered.
Does it sound like a robot reading a menu?
No "press 1, press 2." Callers just say what they need and Kiro answers in plain conversation. It's upfront that it's an assistant — it won't pretend to be a person — and it passes the call to you whenever someone asks for one.
Do I need a new phone or a new number?
No. You keep your current number and your booking book. Kiro sits on the number you already have — nothing to rip out, nothing for your clients to download.
What happens if Kiro can't handle a call?
It hands the call to you with the full context — who's calling and what they need — so no one has to repeat themselves. Kiro is there to catch what you'd miss, not to trap anyone.
Is my call data private?
Your calls and bookings are yours. Kiro handles call data securely and only uses it to run your service. You can read the details in our Privacy Policy.
What does it cost?
Right now we're opening free beta to a handful of salons while we build alongside real owners. No long contract, keep your number, and you can leave whenever you like. When paid plans start, they begin at $179/month.
