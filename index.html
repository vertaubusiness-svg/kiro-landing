Straight with you

What Kiro won't do.

We'd rather tell you the limits up front than oversell you.

  • It won't pretend to be human. Kiro answers plainly as an assistant — and the second a client wants a person, it hands off to you with the full context.
  • It won't trap your clients in a menu. No "press 1, press 2." People say what they need, and Kiro handles it or passes it along.
  • It won't replace your team. Kiro catches the calls you'd otherwise miss. Your stylists still do what they do best.
  • It won't lock you in. No long contract. If Kiro isn't earning its keep, you leave.
