Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Kiro is an AI phone receptionist operated by Kiro Solutions Inc. ("Kiro," "we," "us"), based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. This policy explains what information we collect when you use Kiro or this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who this covers

This policy applies to two groups: businesses that use Kiro to answer their calls, and callers who phone a business that uses Kiro.

Information we collect

  • Account information (from businesses): name, business name, email, phone number, and the settings you configure.
  • Call information (from callers): the caller's phone number, the audio of the call, a written transcript, and any details a caller provides — such as their name, the service they want, and appointment times.
  • Website information: basic technical data such as IP address and, if analytics are enabled, aggregate usage data.

Call recording and transcription

To act as a receptionist, Kiro answers, records, and transcribes incoming calls on behalf of the business using it.

Businesses using Kiro are responsible for providing any call-recording notice or obtaining any consent required by the laws that apply to them and their callers (for example, Canadian privacy law or U.S. state call-recording laws). Kiro provides greetings and disclosures to help meet this requirement.

How we use information

  • To answer calls, book appointments, take messages, and pass calls to the business.
  • To send the business its call details, transcripts, and notifications.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • To communicate with businesses about their account.

We do not sell personal information.

How calls are handled

Calls are answered by an automated AI assistant, not a live person. To provide the service, we use trusted third-party providers — such as telephony, speech-to-text, and AI language providers — who process call data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations, and only to deliver the service.

Where data is processed

Some of our service providers operate in the United States, so information may be processed outside Canada. Where that happens, we remain accountable for the information and take steps to protect it consistent with applicable law.

Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet legal and operational requirements. Businesses can request deletion of their data and their callers' data, subject to any records we must keep by law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard the data in our care.

Your rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA and applicable provincial laws), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us at hello@kirodesk.com. If a caller wishes to exercise rights over data held for a specific business, we may direct the request to that business as the party responsible for the call.

Cookies

This website uses only the cookies needed to function, plus any analytics we may enable to understand traffic. If we add analytics or marketing cookies, we will provide a consent option as required.

Changes

We may update this policy as Kiro grows. We'll change the date above when we do, and material changes will be made clear.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@kirodesk.com or write to Kiro Solutions Inc., Calgary, Alberta, Canada.
